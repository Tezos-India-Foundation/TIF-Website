What is Tezos?

Dedicated to Growing the Tezos Community in India.

The Tezos India Foundation will assist individuals and entities to use the Tezos blockchain. To have the best reach in the country with 1.3 billion people, the Tezos India Foundation will host events in seven different cities. All around India we will give development workshops and assist with the setup of university chapters.
