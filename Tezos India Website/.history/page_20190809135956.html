Dedicated to Growing the Tezos Community in India.

The Tezos India Foundation will assist individuals and entities to use the Tezos blockchain. To have the best reach with 1.3 billion people, the Tezos India Foundation will host events all around India. We will give developer workshops and assist with the setup of university chapters.
